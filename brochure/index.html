About the Program

Road to Spain – India Edition 2026 is a national football talent identification program designed to discover young football talent from across India and provide an international football exposure opportunity in Spain.

Trials run across seven cities in India. Selected players advance to the National Final Camp in Coimbatore, where the final 15 players are chosen to travel to Spain and play in the prestigious Reyes Cup.

The Prize: The Reyes Cup, Spain

The final 15 don't just visit Spain — they compete in the Reyes Cup, a prestigious international youth tournament. International matches, professional coaching, and the chance to be seen by scouts on the world stage.
